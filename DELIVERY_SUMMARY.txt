## 💡 How It Works

### Simple Overview
```
Faculty clicks batch
    ↓
Shows batch details page
    ↓
Displays all students & their documents
    ↓
Shows status & similarity scores
```

### Technical Flow
```
BatchDetailPage
    ↓
Calls: ApiService.getBatchDetails()
    ↓
POST: /api/get-batch-details/
    ↓
Backend: Fetches Batch, StudentBatchMapping, Documents
    ↓
Returns: JSON with complete data
    ↓
Display: Batch info + Student table + Detail cards
```
